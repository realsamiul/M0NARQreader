The Tk 5,000+ Crore Market: Our Sector-by-Sector Execution Plan.

Our technology unlocks value across Bangladesh's five most critical sectors. We have a targeted plan for entry, starting with Banking and the high-value government contract for Land Records.

The Addressable Market
Banking (TAM: Tk 11.4 Cr/Year)
Solving 15M Cheques/year and 66M Account Opening Forms/year. Our 97% accuracy replaces 15-50 minutes of manual work with a 3-second scan.
Land Records (Contract: Tk 47.5 Cr)
The government's UNDP pilot (Aug 2025) needs to digitize 8.5M+ aged, handwritten deeds. Our 92% handwritten accuracy makes us the *only* viable technical partner.
Healthcare (TAM: Tk 950 Cr)
The SHR 2025-26 project (Tk 1,770 Cr budget) aims to digitize 180M patient visits/year. Our platform is the only one capable of reading doctors' cursive and 100-page reports at scale.
Government (Infra Play: Tk 40 Cr/Year)
We are the enabling infrastructure for the National Data Exchange (NDX). We turn unstructured paper from all ministries (Land, Health, Banking) into the clean, validated JSON data the NDX requires.
Insurance (High-ROI: Tk 6 Cr/Year)
Automating 350K claims/year. Our cross-validation and fraud engine can reduce the 5-8% fraud rate to <1%, saving insurers crores annually.
